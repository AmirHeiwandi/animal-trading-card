Lion

Since the beginning of time, the lion has been the king of the jungle. Known for its excellent leadership and respectful character.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Lenth: 196 centimeters
  • Average Weight: 205 kg
  • Habitat: Jungle

A predator on four, ruler of the jungle. Distinctive beard showing the dominant side. The inspiration of the classic Disney movie "The Lion King".
